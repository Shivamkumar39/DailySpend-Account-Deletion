How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your DailySpend account and associated data, send an email to [YOUR SUPPORT EMAIL].

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Registered email address
  • App name: DailySpend
  • Subject line: Delete my account

What data will be deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Expense records
  • Budget data
  • Category data

What data may be retained temporarily

Certain data may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Security monitoring
  • Fraud prevention
  • Legal or regulatory compliance

Processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days of verification.

Important note

If additional information is required to verify account ownership, we may contact you before processing the deletion request.
