# DailySpend Account Deletion

If you want to delete your DailySpend account and associated data,
please send a request to: your-email@example.com

Include:
- Registered email ID
- App name: DailySpend
- Subject: Delete my account

What will be deleted:
- Account profile
- Expense records
- Budget data
- Category data

What may be retained temporarily:
- Data required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance, if applicable.

Request processing time:
- Within 7 business days
